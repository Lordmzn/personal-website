Research Areas

  • I worked on designing operations of multipurposes water reservoirs using multi objective genetic algorithms and dynamic systems.
  • I have coworked on the creation of a riverscape evolution model based on optimality principle for the explanation and prediction of basin scale landscape evolution.
  • I have studied indicators to define the alteration of an hydrologic regime so to provide an assessment of impacts of reservoirs operation on downstream ecological units.
